<p>
  <code>notifySlack</code> posts a colour-coded build status message to Slack via
  an incoming webhook. The payload is rendered from the bundled
  <code>slack-message.json</code> resource template.
</p>

<p><strong>Usage</strong></p>
<pre>
notifySlack(
    channel:       '#deployments',
    status:        'SUCCESS',
    message:       'payments-api 1.4.0 deployed',
    credentialsId: 'slack-webhook'
)
</pre>

<p>
  The webhook URL is read from a Jenkins <em>Secret text</em> credential named by
  <code>credentialsId</code> (default <code>slack-webhook</code>).
</p>
