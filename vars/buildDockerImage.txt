<p>
  <code>buildDockerImage</code> builds a Docker image and optionally logs in to a
  registry and pushes it.
</p>

<p><strong>Usage</strong></p>
<pre>
buildDockerImage(
    image:         'registry.example.com/payments-api',
    tag:           '1.4.0',
    dockerfile:    'Dockerfile',
    push:          true,
    credentialsId: 'registry-creds'
)
</pre>

<p>
  Returns the fully-qualified <code>image:tag</code> reference that was built.
</p>
