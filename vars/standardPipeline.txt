<p>
  <code>standardPipeline</code> provides a complete, opinionated declarative
  pipeline covering checkout, unit tests, image build and optional deploy.
</p>

<p><strong>Usage</strong></p>
<pre>
standardPipeline(
    appName:      'payments-api',
    registry:     'registry.example.com',
    slackChannel: '#deployments',
    deployBranch: 'main',
    deployEnv:    'production'
)
</pre>

<p>
  All keys are optional except <code>appName</code>. Overrides are merged onto
  the defaults defined in <code>org.acme.jenkins.Config</code>.
</p>
