<p>
  <code>runUnitTests</code> runs the project's unit tests for a supported build
  tool (<code>gradle</code>, <code>maven</code> or <code>npm</code>) and always
  publishes the JUnit XML results, even on failure.
</p>

<p><strong>Usage</strong></p>
<pre>
runUnitTests(tool: 'gradle', coverage: true)
</pre>
